Visiting France

Museums in


Paris

A trip to the Louvre can take ages even before you step foot inside. Avoid long lines by buying timed tickets as soon as you can—most go on sale about two months in advance. If you spring for a Paris Museum Pass, which gives free entry to dozens of museums and galleries, you’ll still need to book timed tickets. You can also save yourself the hassle by opting for a guided tour with skip-the-line access. Guides know all the tricks for seeing blockbuster works like the Mona Lisa as efficiently as possible, and they know other popular museums around the city, from the Musée d'Orsay to the Musée Rodin, inside and out.
